The Best Animes According to Daniel

My Top 3 Animes of All-time

"Your Name"

This movie is being directed by Makoto Shinkai. It has very stunning visual as an anime and very beautiful story.

"Violet Evergarden"

This movie tells the story of a woman who tries her best to discover what love means. Make sure you prepare your heart before watching this one, cause I cried a lot.

"Silent Voice"

This movie have a wonderful story and visual. The end of the anime taught me to learn to forgive my past mistakes.
